★ What ATH means
  • ATH = All-Time High - best ever for that program
  • These rare windows may not repeat for years
  • Current bonuses flagged ATH when they match the record
  • Past ATH records preserved in Bonus History
Transfer ratio notes
  • Most programs transfer at 1:1
  • Amex to AeroMexico: 1:1.6 - you gain 60% more
  • Amex to Cathay Pacific: 5:4 (20% loss)
  • Capital One to JAL: 2:1.5 (25% loss)
  • Hilton via Amex: 1:2 - each point = 2 Hilton pts
Transfer speed
  • Instant: Chase UR to most partners
  • ~1 hr: Citi to Etihad
  • 12-24 hrs: Amex to Iberia, Singapore, Qatar
  • 1-2 days: Capital One to Cathay, Singapore
  • 3-7 days: Citi to Thai Airways - plan ahead
